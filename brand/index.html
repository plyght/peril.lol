Usage

The logo and flag are available as SVG. Always maintain clear space equal to at least the height of the crossbar around all marks.

The wordmark is always set in lowercase EB Garamond at weight 400. Do not bold, italicize, or alter letterforms.

When placing on colored backgrounds, use the inverted variant to maintain contrast.
