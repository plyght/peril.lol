The concept of following decoupled reciprocity of interest, replaced it with the kind of asymmetry commerce requires. Social structures became algorithms: accumulators of marketable attention. Publishing power was artificially centralized in a decentralized system. Let's not emphasize what we see, but how we see it. A photography of perspective, of earthy gut-feeling laced with aesthetic curiosity. Photography as a way of making places and inventing language. Let's publish the way we see, among friends.

Self-publishing

Digital self-publishing requires no centralized platforms. It is open to everyone willing to acquire basic knowledge and spend a modicum of money. Perceive the web as the web of subjective zines. Tell me about yours.
