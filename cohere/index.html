Cohere CLI

Welcome to Cohere CLI, your gateway to AI-powered interactions.

$ cohere --help

Available commands:

  • /web <query> - Perform a web search
  • /chat - Start an AI conversation
  • /clear - Clear the terminal
  • :q - Quit the current session
$ cohere /web "latest AI trends"
Searching for latest AI trends... 1. Large Language Models (LLMs) 2. Multimodal AI 3. AI-assisted coding 4. Ethical AI and bias mitigation
$ cohere /chat
